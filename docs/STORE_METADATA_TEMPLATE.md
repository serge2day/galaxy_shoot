## Long Description
Galaxy Shooter is a classic vertical arcade space shooter built for mobile.

Pilot one of three unique ships through three handcrafted stages, each ending with an epic boss fight. Defeat waves of enemy drones, interceptors, and gunships while collecting power-ups to boost your weapons, shield, and health.

FEATURES:
- 3 unique stages with distinct enemies and bosses
- 3 playable ships with different stats and play styles
- 3 difficulty tiers: Normal, Veteran, and Expert
- Permanent upgrades: boost HP, fire rate, damage, and speed
- Persistent progression: earn credits, unlock ships, clear stages
- Smooth drag-to-move controls optimized for mobile
- Auto-fire and manual fire modes
- Fully offline - no ads, no accounts, no data collection

Designed for quick pick-up-and-play sessions. No internet required.
